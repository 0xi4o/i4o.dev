I've been getting into Remix recently, and decided to move one of my Next.js projects to Remix. The move should be straight forward with the exception of authentication. Or so I thought.

With Next.js, implementing authentication was pretty easy with next-auth. I just have to pick the auth providers I need, pass in the required API keys and secrets, wrap our `_app` component in a `SessionProvider`, and we're done. Next Auth takes care of everything else.
